Services

Talent Marketing

At PSM, we understand that talent marketing is more than just promotion—it's about building a legacy. Our approach is centered on showcasing the unique qualities, skills, and achievements of our clients, ensuring they reach the right audience at the right time. From elite athletes to cultural influencers, we create tailored strategies that elevate our clients' brands and open doors to exciting opportunities.

Strategic Partnerships

At PSM, we believe in creating long-term relationships, not just transactions. Our focus is on building brand equity and ensuring that every partnership we forge enhances our clients' personal and professional narratives. With our connections to some of the most recognized brands in the world, we deliver strategic partnerships that resonate and drive sustained value.

Brand Building & Endorsements

We go beyond traditional marketing by crafting sophisticated strategies that amplify our clients' brands. This includes everything from endorsements and licensing to personal appearances and speaking engagements. We work closely with our clients to develop authentic and aligned brand narratives, facilitating introductions to national and international brands for endorsement opportunities that reflect their passions.

Media & Public Relations at PSM

At PSM, we craft and manage your public image to ensure your story resonates. Through strategic media relations and targeted outreach, we position our clients as influential voices in their fields. Our extensive network of global media contacts allows us to secure impactful coverage, highlighting your achievements and enhancing your reputation.

Events

PSM specializes in creating unforgettable live and virtual events. From ideation to execution, we handle every detail, including event management, partner alignment, and vendor selection. Whether it's a large-scale event or a unique passion project, we bring your vision to life with precision and creativity.

Business Ventures

At PSM, we empower our clients to explore new entrepreneurial opportunities. From market research and strategic planning to financial modeling and venture partnerships, we provide the guidance and resources needed to turn innovative ideas into successful business ventures. Whether it's in sports tech, media, or other emerging industries, PSM is here to support every step of the journey.

Community Relations

At PSM, we believe in the power of athletes to inspire and give back. We help our clients engage with their communities through charitable events, partnerships, and local initiatives. Whether it’s launching a foundation, supporting a cause, or organizing community outreach, we guide our clients in making a lasting positive impact.

NIL

PSM is committed to helping collegiate athletes maximize their Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) opportunities. We offer comprehensive NIL advisory services, guiding athletes through the complexities of NIL deals and partnerships. From negotiating endorsements to managing time and maximizing value, PSM ensures that our clients are empowered to build their brand and seize every opportunity.

Memorabilia Signings

At PSM, we broker high-profile sports memorabilia signings, both public and private. Leveraging our extensive network and industry expertise, we connect athletes with opportunities through a variety of exclusive signing events. Whether it’s a public signing with fans or a private session, we handle the logistics and coordination, ensuring a seamless and successful experience every time.
